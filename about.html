Our Story

A culinary journey since 2010

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Bellini, Savory Bites began as a small 12-seat bistro with a simple mission: to create memorable dining experiences through exceptional food and warm hospitality.

What started as a neighborhood gem quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to traditional recipes, earning a Michelin Bib Gourmand in 2015.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing culinary innovation, sourcing ingredients from local farmers and artisans who share our commitment to quality.
